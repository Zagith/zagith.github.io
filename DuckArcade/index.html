Welcome to Duck Arcade (Pre-Alpha)!

Instructions:

To control the duck, simply keep push left-click on the screen, arrows will appear around the mouse point.

Hover your cursor over these arrows to move the duck in the desired direction.

Objective:

The goal is to color the tiles by passing over them or defeat enemies strategically placed on the grid.

Watch out for monsters and use your wit to outmaneuver them. Good luck and have fun!"

Monster Legend:

Red Chicken: Run away! It's fast and aggressive.

Regular Chicken: Defeat it by moving over its tile.

Turret: Run away as fast as you can to avoid its attacks.
